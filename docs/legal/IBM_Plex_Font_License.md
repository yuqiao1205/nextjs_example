IBM Plex fonts are licensed under the **SIL Open Font License (OFL) Version 1.1**.

## Summary of Terms

The SIL Open Font License is a free, libre, and open-source license specifically designed for fonts and related software. It allows the fonts to be used, studied, modified, and redistributed freely as long as they are not sold by themselves.

## Permitted Uses

The IBM Plex fonts may be used for:

- **Commercial Projects**: Full permission to use in commercial applications, websites, and products
- **Personal Projects**: Free use in any personal or non-commercial work

- **Redistribution**: The fonts may be bundled with software or distributed as part of larger projects

## Key Conditions

When using IBM Plex fonts under the OFL 1.1 license, the following conditions apply:

1. **Attribution**: The copyright notice and license must be included with any distribution of the font files
2. **No Standalone Sales**: The fonts cannot be sold by themselves; they must be bundled with software or other content
3. **Name Reservation**: Modified versions must not use the reserved font name "IBM Plex" unless explicit permission is granted
4. **License Preservation**: Any redistribution must include the original license and copyright notices
